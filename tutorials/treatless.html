    • Treats & Treatless FAQ

    What are treats?

    Treats are fancreations that you receive in addition to the one which was assigned to be created for you. They are extras! They are still required to be a ship you've asked for and to follow your DNWs, but they can be much shorter or may only be sketches instead of clean lineart. Treats can be gifted to more than one person; for example, if four people have requested Sebastian Vael/Female Hawke/Varric Tethras, and you've drawn fanart which doesn't violate any of their DNW, you can gift that to all four recipients.

    You could receive one treat, multiple treats, or no treats.

    Treats can also be given to the exchange as a whole, by placing them in the collection, and NOT completing the "Gift to" section. You can treat the collection with ships which are on the tagslist but didn't make it to the requests, creations which don't quite fit a single prompt (maybe the ships were requested but there are DNWs in your creation), or treats which fit so many prompts to gift it directly to so many participants would be cumbersome.

    Who are the Treatless?

    Treatless partcipants are ones who haven't gotten at least one treat. Many exchange participants like to make sure everyone gets a treat, so they work hard to clear the Treatless List, by writing or drawing more than one piece during the creation period. "Treatless" is just our way of identifying those who haven't gotten an extra yet.

    Where is the Treatless List?

    We're experimenting! Many exchanges create separate Google Sheets spreadsheet which list all of the treatless individuals and may or may not link to their requests. The AutoAO3 app which we're using for our requests spreadsheet allows a lot of cool sorting and filtering tricks, and we're hoping to use that to compile our treatless list.

    We added a tag to our freeforms tags. Instead of just the normal "fanart" and "fanfic" designations, we've also added "treatless". Participants who are interested in receiving treats are being asked to click this on their requests so ensure they are included on the list. As treats come in, the mods will be editing participants' requests to remove this tag and then rescraping the info on the list to update it.

    This is cool and all, but... I don't want treats.

    We understand. We ask that you do two things in order to help make sure you don't receive treats:

    1. Make sure you do not click "Treatless" on your requests. If you never appear on the list it will make it less likely for you to receive treats.
    2. Please also list "treats" in your DNWs. Some people will treat anyone who's prompt inspires them whether they're on the treatless list or not; this helps ensure that if anyone is really inspired by your requests, they will not write it unless they were assigned it.
    If you still receive a treat after taking both of the above steps, please reach out to a moderator to let us know. You may always refuse the gift in AO3, but it will remain in the collection as a treat to the exchange as a whole.
