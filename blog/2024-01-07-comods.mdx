For 2024, we'd like to add co-mods to our events (currently
[Arlathan eXchange](https://arlathanxchange.neocities.org) and
[DA Poly Exchange](https://dapolyshipping.neocities.org)).

Last year it became obvious to us that continuing to run two exchanges a year
(as well as mod Dragon Age Annual) would make it impossible for us to continue
the events long-term without serious detriment to ourselves. Although there's no
plans to stop either exchange _yet_ we wanted to ask for help **before** it got
that bad.

**We're looking for social media and advertising help**, in particular, but if
you're interested in other aspects of modding (like managing tag sets, AO3
collections, our website, etc) we'd love to have help with that too. _We're
willing to teach you anything you don't currently know_ about running exchanges.

Anyone who joins the team will be an equal moderator with the current
moderators.

## Requirements

- 18+
- Dragon Age Fan
- No history of harassment of other fans or Bioware personnel
- Willingness to adhere to the guidelines in the
  [Mod Info](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1FX8dHTQjeLVSiiWuVIP--3ONUgMmqLsg4JmV_nGWmlI/edit?usp=sharing)

## Not Required

- Participation in events
- Moderating **both/all** events
- Prior mod experience
- Coding experience
- AO3 exchange experience
