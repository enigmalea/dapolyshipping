**To Make Corrections:**

1. Go to the[ 2023 Tag Set.](https://archiveofourown.org/tag_sets/16282)
2. Click the My Nominations button on the top right of the page under the menu
   bar.
3. Click the Edit button on the top right of the page under the menu bar.
4. Click the X to the right of the tag, and reenter the corrected tag in the
   text box that appears.
5. Click the Submit button at the bottom of the page.
