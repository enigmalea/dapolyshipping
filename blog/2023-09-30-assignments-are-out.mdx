Apologies for the late announcement. Assignments went out to everyone who
signed-up on Monday, and you should have received an email. If, for some reason,
you didn't, please contact the moderators immediately.

## Reminders

**Creation**

- You need to create one piece for your assigned recipient which should feature
  healthy polyamory.
- Your piece should focus on one of the requested ships.
- You do not have to create any prompts provided, but should avoid any Do Not
  Wants while hopefully hitting a like or two (if provided).
- See [The Works](/docs/exchange/intro#the-works) and [FAQs](/docs/exchange/faq)
  for more info.

**Assignments Help**

- If there's a problem with your assignment, please contact the moderators ASAP.
- If you have questions for your recipient, you can ask the moderators to
  contact them for you.
- Relatedly, our primary way to contact you if we can't match your AO3 name to
  your Discord (or you haven't joined our Discord) will be via **email**. Please
  check your AO3 emails relatively frequently throughout the exchange!
- If you find you need an extension, you may contact us directly or complete
  [Our Extension Form](../../extensions).
- If you find you'll be unable to complete your assignment in time for any
  reason, please let us know _prior_ to defaulting on AO3. We do not necessarily
  have to know why you're defaulting, but would like a heads-up as we've had
  people default accidentally in the past.

**MOST IMPORTANTLY:** Have fun creating! We're looking forward to seeing
everything you're working on!
