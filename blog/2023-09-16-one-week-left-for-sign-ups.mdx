## An Important Note

We've received two emails about this and realized we forgot to mention it for
this exchange (though we mentioned it during our our exchange
[Arlathan eXchange](https://arlathanxchange.neocities.org)):

> there is a bug on AO3 which impacts editing your sign-ups. We've edited the
> [Tutorials: How to Sign-Up](/docs/exchange/tutorials/signups) to include the
> warning below.

:::warning

Be very careful if you edit your sign-ups. During an exchange last year we
noticed a bug where more "Additional Tags" can be selected on _Requests_ when
someone is editing. For example, Fanart may be added to your request, even
though you have only requested Fanfic. Please review your requests and offers
_very carefully_ after you edit.

:::
