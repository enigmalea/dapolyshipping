Some Notes On Tagging:

we tag by type of content (fic, art, etc), game, rating, canon characters, ocs, ships, and AUs/tropes.

we use full names for games (i.e. Dragon Age: Origins). For content focused on the unnamed new game, we are using DA4 as a tag. It will be updated once the name is released.

all fics are tagged by rating, so that you can filter out rated e or rated m content if you would like. please remember that rated m and e content may not be sexually explicit, but may be tagged that for other reasons (violence, mature themes, etc).

all sexually explicit material is also tagged #lemons so you may filter that content if wanted.

canon characters are tagged using first and last name (if available). The Iron Bull is listed as Iron Bull. The suspected female qunari companion from DA4 is nicknamed Harley Qun (based on this tweet) until her name is revealed. Fen'Harel is listed separate from Solas for content where he is a separate entity (via time travel, magic, or other means).

OCs are identified by gender and last name (i.e. m!Aeducan or f!Hawke). Our initial tags are based on context clues, tags from your post, or tags from AO3, but if we misidentify your OC feel free to drop us an ask or send us a message.

nonbinary and trans OCs may be listed as nb!lastname or trans!lastname in addition to their gender if you would like in case someone is looking for that in particular.

OCs without a canon counterpart (i.e. not a canon Hero of Fereldan or Inquisitor) are tagged f!oc or m!oc.

currently the only aus being tagged for are modern au and victorian au, but if we see others that people may be interested in (or may want to avoid) we'll tag those as well.
